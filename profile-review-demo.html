Questions

Good to know.

Is it really free?

Yes — completely free, on the free plan or Premium. We don't charge for feedback and never will. It's part of how we keep our community strong.

Do I need to be on Premium?

No. Anyone can request a review. But on the free plan a reviewer only sees one photo, so the notes are limited. Premium lets them watch your reel and full gallery — which makes the review far more useful.

Will it be by email or in person?

Most reviews are delivered by email, since the casting directors we work with keep tight schedules. In-person sessions at our Times Square office happen when availability allows. CastSlate decides the format.

How long does it take?

Up to about a week. We get a high volume of requests and each one is reviewed by a real person, so thank you for your patience.

Where do I find my profile link?

In your Dashboard there's a Copy link button on your profile — tap it, then paste the link into the form above.
