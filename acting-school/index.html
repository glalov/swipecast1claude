About George

The Insider Who Teaches You to Win the Room

With 32+ major productions under his belt — from studio blockbusters to Emmy-watched TV — George Ludlow brings a perspective no standard acting coach can offer: the view from the other side of the casting table.

"I've sat across from actors on Alien: Romulus, Wednesday, Percy Jackson, Barbarian, and dozens more. I built this curriculum around exactly what we look for — and what costs people the job."

Having worked as casting assistant, casting associate, casting coordinator, and casting producer across Netflix, Disney+, HBO, and major studio features, George knows the difference between an actor who is talented and an actor who books. His classes close that gap.
