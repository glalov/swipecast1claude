Featured Class Banner — Scroll-Collapse Demo

Inspired by the Backstage.com behavior: a full-width promo banner appears immediately above the navigation, then smoothly collapses into a thinner sticky strip as the visitor scrolls — without ever fully disappearing or blocking the nav.

How to test: scroll inside each frame below. The banner starts full-height, then shrinks into a slim sticky strip once you scroll past it, and expands back to full size when you scroll back to the top. Nothing here is live yet — this is a preview only. Each frame is an independent scroll container simulating the page viewport (desktop ~760px, mobile ~340px).
