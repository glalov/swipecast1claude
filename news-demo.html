What changed in this version
  • Compact & subordinate — a single tidy row of 4 small cards (wraps to 2 on tablet/mobile), tucked between your main features and the footer. It deliberately doesn't compete with casting calls, profiles, pricing, or join CTAs.
  • Master Show / Hide toggle — first control in Super Admin → News Controls. Flip it in this demo and watch the section vanish cleanly (no empty gap). If it ever feels messy or thin, hide it — no funeral required.
  • Click a card → full CastSlate article page: image, title, date, category, original staff-written summary, credited source link + legal disclaimer.
  • Still legally safe — generated placeholder art, summaries in our own words, source link on every story. No Backstage anywhere.
